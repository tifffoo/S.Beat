How do I use this website?:
With Juxtapost, you can easily access and compare news articles from different news outlets all across the world based on a specific search term. This can help you more easily formulate your opinions on the similarities and differences between different news sources or even different countries on how they report on a certain topic.

So...what's the point?:
Many people may unknowingly find themselves in a "filter bubble", in which they are only exposed to content that aligns with their beliefs and/or values. This eventually leads to becoming isolated or unaware of differing opinions, thus being placed within a "bubble". With Juxtapost, we hope to let you more easily step out of that bubble by allowing you to more easily compare all different kinds of news, and broaden your perspective about what kind of content you're consuming.

Influenze (Flu)

The flu is, like the common cold, a viral infection. The symptoms are often a more severe form of those found in common colds. Proper rest and care will be helpful in assisting recovery.

Symptoms include:

  • fever
  • cough
  • sore throat
  • runny or stuffy nose
  • muscle or body aches
  • headaches
  • fatigue

Treatments include:

The flu is slightly worse than the common cold, so these tips may help in easing your symptoms.

  • Drink plenty of liquids. Water, juice, and soup will help keep you hydrated.
  • Rest! Sleeping allows your body to fight off infection.
  • Over-the-counter pain relievers such as acetaminophen (Tylenol, others) or ibuprofen (Advil, Motrin IB, others) may soothe headaches. Only use when needed.

Seeing a Doctor:

If you feel your symptoms require medical attention, see a doctor. They may perscribe you an antiviral medication like Tamiflu or Relenza. These may lessen your illness by a day.

Norovirus

Norovirus is a foodborne illness, meaning it spreads from contiminated food and/or water. With the large volume of students in small spaces and everyone sharing bathrooms and dining halls, this illness is very easily spread.

Symptoms include:

  • low grade fever
  • chills
  • headache
  • muscle aches
  • fatigue
  • diarrhea
  • vomiting
  • dehydration

Treatments include:

There is no medicine to cure Norovirus (the illness does not respond to antibiotics), but there are some things you can do to start up on your recovery.

  • Drink extra water. Norovirus can make you very dehydrated, so make sure to replace those fluids as best as possible
  • Avoid sugary drinks to lessen diarrhea
